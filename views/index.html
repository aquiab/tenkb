10kb

this is an image gallery where anyone can upload images that are under 10kbs in size

inspired by:

  • nostalgia for old internet forums and the internet from the 2000s
  • the 20kbps music label
  • an interest in how the internet would turn out to be if disk storage became scarcer
  • the 10k apart contest
  • webcore and animecore visual aesthetics
  • the one terabyte of kilobyte age project


if your images and gifs are over the size limit, you can compress them here: static images, gifs, resizing them to a lower size is also a good idea
